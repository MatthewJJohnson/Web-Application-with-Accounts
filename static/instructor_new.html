Instructor Sign Up

Creating an instructor account will allow you to list the courses for which you are in need of TAs. Once your courses are listed, TAs may apply to be a TA for one or more of the courses you will be instructing. Once TAs have applied you may choose which TA(s) will be assigned to your courses. As more TAs apply, you can change who the selected TA(s) for your courses are.
